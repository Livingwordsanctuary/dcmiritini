Welcome to church where:

  • The Word of God is taught and forms the basis of all we say or do.
  • Your talents, gifts and abilities are recognized and utilized.
  • All members are individually and collectively important and are treated as such.
  • Your all round growth in the concern of the Pastor and other church leaders.

Core values:

  • Reaching people with the Gospel of Christ through continuous evangelism.
  • Sincere individual and collective love for Christ.
  • ASincere interpersonal christian fellowship
  • Growing in Christian maturity, integrity and character.
  • Upholding the Bible as the infallible Word of God.
